SEQyC

  • Supporting the reliable management and interpretation of chemical data
    At SeQyC, we work to:
    · Disseminate knowledge of chemometrics and qualimetry in academic and professional contexts.
    · Promote the interdisciplinary use of chemometrics by integrating new perspectives and approaches.
    · Create an active network for knowledge exchange and collaboration among our members.
    · Strengthen collaboration with international scientific societies.
    · Establish an open-access platform.
    · Support the training and advancement of young researchers.
